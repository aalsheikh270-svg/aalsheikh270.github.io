Custom Fit Studio

Experience the perfect fit. Our advanced 3D scanning technology creates footwear designed exclusively for your unique foot geometry. No compromises. No discomfort. Just pure performance.

The Future of Footwear

At AeroStride's Custom Fit Studio, we believe that every athlete deserves footwear that fits perfectly. Using state-of-the-art 3D scanning technology, we capture over 1 million data points from your feet to create shoes that are uniquely yours.

Our custom fitting process goes beyond simple sizing. We analyze your foot shape, arch height, pressure points, and biomechanics to design footwear that enhances your performance and prevents injuries. Whether you're a professional athlete or a weekend warrior, our Custom Fit Studio delivers precision engineering tailored to your body.

How It Works

01

Book Your Visit

Schedule an appointment at our Custom Fit Studio. Each session takes approximately 60-90 minutes.

02

3D Foot Scan

Our advanced scanner captures precise measurements of both feet, including length, width, arch height, and pressure distribution.

03

Gait Analysis

Walk and run on our specialized treadmill while our motion capture technology analyzes your biomechanics.

04

Design Consultation

Work with our fit specialists to choose materials, colors, and features that match your sport and style.

05

Precision Crafting

Your shoes are handcrafted by master craftsmen using your exact specifications and premium materials.

06

Delivery & Fitting

Receive your custom shoes in 4-6 weeks. Return for a final fitting to ensure perfection.
