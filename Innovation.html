THERMOLACE

Self-Adjusting Technology

Unique Selling Proposition
The world's first self-adjusting lace system that intelligently responds to body heat for optimal comfort during activity and releases with sensor-enabled cooling cloth in 3 seconds.
01
Innovation Overview

Revolutionary Technology

ThermoLace is a revolutionary self contracting lace system that utilizes advanced shape memory polymer technology combined with heat responsive materials. The laces automatically tighten when they detect body heat generated during physical activity, providing optimal support and comfort throughout your performance.

When you're ready to remove your footwear, simply place the included cooling cloth over the laces for 3 seconds, and they instantly loosen. The system consists of heat responsive laces made from shape memory polymers, and a specially designed cooling cloth with embedded sensors that trigger the release mechanism.
