03
Strategic Actions

Commitments In Motion

01

Ocean to Shoe Program

Converting ocean bound plastic waste into high performance materials. Goal: 90% of all textiles sourced from recovered marine debris and post industrial waste by 2029.

  • Coastal cleanup partnerships in 12 countries
  • Algae based foam cushioning in development
  • Compostable shoeboxes from agricultural waste
02

Level the Field Initiative

Eliminating economic barriers to athletic excellence through full equipment grants, nutrition programs, and professional coaching for 20,000+ youth from low-income families annually.

  • 42 year round training centers in urban communities
  • 1,200+ certified coaches and sports nutritionists
  • College prep workshops and scholarship placement services
03

Green Energy Transition

Complete conversion to renewable energy across all owned facilities by 2027, plus direct investment in wind and solar infrastructure to offset partner manufacturing emissions.

  • 150,000+ solar panels installed at global facilities
  • Wind farm investments generating 80MW annually
  • Electric vehicle fleet for all distribution operations
04

Gear Revival Network

"Give It Another Mile" program refurbishes returned footwear to like new condition for donation to athletes in need, with materials from unsalvageable products recycled into new goods.

  • 80,000+ pairs restored and redistributed each year
  • Global network of 350+ nonprofit receiving partners
  • 100% diversion rate from landfills and incinerators
